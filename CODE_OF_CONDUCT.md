# Code of Conduct
OpenRabbit Platform is committed to a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free community.

## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume positive intent and collaborate in good faith.
- Provide actionable technical feedback.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Public or private intimidation.
- Sharing private information without permission.

## Enforcement
Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code of conduct and may remove or reject contributions that violate these standards.
